# DTMA Platform Prototype - Access Guide

Hi Team,

I'm excited to share the progress on the DTMA (Digital Transformation Maturity Academy) platform prototype. The platform is now ready for review and testing.

## 🌐 Accessing the Platform

**Landing Page URL:** http://localhost:8080

The landing page showcases the complete DTMA experience including:
- Hero section with AI-powered search
- Featured courses across all 6 digital dimensions
- Faculty profiles (Human + AI instructors)
- 6XD framework overview
- Persona-based learning paths
- Course marketplace

## 👤 Learner Dashboard Access

To access the learner dashboard and explore the student experience:

1. Navigate to the landing page (http://localhost:8080)
2. Click "Sign In" in the top navigation bar
3. Create a new account by clicking "Sign up" on the authentication page
4. Fill in your details and complete registration
5. Once logged in, you'll be redirected to the Learner Dashboard

**Learner Dashboard Features:**
- Progress tracking and course enrollment
- Learning player with video content
- Assessments and quizzes
- Assignments and credentials
- Gamification features (points, badges, leaderboards)
- Live classes and notifications
- Collaboration tools
- Profile management
- Butler AI assistant for personalized support

## 🔧 Admin Dashboard Access

To access the admin side of the platform:

1. Scroll to the bottom of any page to the footer
2. Click on the "Admin" link in the footer
3. You'll be taken directly to the Admin Dashboard (no authentication required for prototype)

**Admin Dashboard Features:**
- Course management and creation
- Assessment and quiz builder
- Training delivery tools
- Enrollment management
- Faculty management
- Communication tools
- Governance and compliance
- Organization management
- Certification management
- Commerce and payment settings
- System settings
