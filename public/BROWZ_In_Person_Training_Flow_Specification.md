# BROWZ Academy -- In-Person Training Flow Specification

## Overview

This document defines the user experience flow for the new **In-Person
Masterclasses** section added to the landing page, positioned before the
Testimonials section.

The goal is to clearly differentiate between: - On-demand online
courses - Date-based, seat-limited in-person training sessions

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Entry Point -- Landing Page Hybrid Section

## Section Headline

**Theory Builds Knowledge. Practice Builds Confidence.**

At BROWZ, learning doesn't stop at the screen.

Our in-person masterclasses give you the hands-on exposure, clinical
awareness, and guided refinement needed to operate with confidence in
real-world settings.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Primary User Actions

Users can:

1.  Click **View All Upcoming Sessions**
2.  Click **Reserve Your Seat / View Details** on a specific session
    card

Each action follows a distinct but connected flow.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Flow: View All Upcoming Sessions

## Step 1 → Masterclasses Listing Page

This page is event-based, not course-based.

### Page Structure

### Header

In-Person Masterclasses\
Hands-on, expert-led, seat-limited training sessions.

### Filters

-   Location
-   Category
-   Date
-   Instructor
-   Availability

### Session Cards (Grid or List)

Each card must display: - Session Title - Location - Date - Duration -
Instructor Name - Seats Remaining Indicator - Price - CTA: View Details

### Sorting Priority

1.  Upcoming soonest
2.  Highest demand
3.  Limited seats

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Flow: View Details (Session Detail Page)

## Step 2 → Masterclass Detail Page

This page should feel more premium than an online course page.

### Page Structure

### 1. Hero Section

-   Session title
-   Location
-   Date
-   Instructor
-   Seats remaining
-   Price
-   Sticky "Reserve Your Seat" button

### 2. What You'll Learn

-   Practical skills
-   Techniques covered
-   Tools used

### 3. Who It's For

-   Skill level
-   Pre-requisites
-   Required certifications (if applicable)

### 4. What's Included

-   Certification
-   Materials
-   Practice kit
-   Meals (if applicable)

### 5. Venue Details

-   Address
-   Map embed
-   Parking information

### 6. Instructor Profile

-   Bio
-   Credentials
-   Verification badge

### 7. Seats & Availability

-   Live counter
-   Scarcity messaging (e.g., "Only 5 seats left")

### 8. FAQ

-   Refund policy
-   Transfer policy
-   What to bring

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Booking Flow

## Step 3 → Booking Action

User clicks **Reserve Your Seat**

### If logged out:

-   Prompt login or account creation
-   Redirect back to booking

### If logged in:

-   Proceed directly to booking form

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 4 → Booking Confirmation Page

Collect: - Attendance confirmation - Agreement to terms - Pre-session
questionnaire (optional)

Proceed to payment.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 5 → Payment

Options: - Full payment - Deposit + remaining balance (future
enhancement)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Step 6 → Confirmation Page

Display: - Booking confirmation message - Calendar download (.ics) - Add
to Google Calendar - Email confirmation notice - Pre-session checklist

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Dashboard Integration

Add new learner dashboard tab:

## My Sessions

Display: - Upcoming sessions - Completed sessions - Certificates
(downloadable)

This ensures unified platform experience between online and in-person
training.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Psychological & UX Principles

In-person sessions must feel:

-   Seat-limited
-   Date-specific
-   Instructor-authoritative
-   Commitment-based (not casual purchase)

Key UX Requirements: - Clear location display - Clear date visibility -
Scarcity indicators - Refund transparency - Sticky booking CTA

------------------------------------------------------------------------
