AI Capabilities for Admin, Faculty & Operations
1. AI Assistant for Internal Operations

Purpose:
A general-purpose assistant embedded in admin dashboards to support daily operational work.

Core functions:

Summarize platform activity

Generate operational reports

Suggest next-best actions for admins and faculty

Draft responses to learner queries

2. Transact AI – Faculty Support Mode

This extends the learner-facing Transact AI into internal support mode, allowing faculty and staff to:

View learner progress summaries

Receive AI-generated mentoring suggestions

Get guidance on how to respond to struggling learners

See recommended interventions or outreach actions

3. AI Content Authoring & Drafting

Supports instructional designers and faculty during course creation.

Capabilities:

Draft lesson outlines

Generate examples and exercises

Propose learning objectives

Suggest improvements to existing content

This accelerates course development while keeping humans in control.

4. AI Assessment Tools (Admin Side)
AI Quiz Generator

Create quizzes from lesson content

Suggest question variations and difficulty levels

AI Grading Helper

Assist in grading open-ended responses

Highlight key points and rubric matches

Suggest provisional scores for instructor review

5. AI Cohort Risk & Training Needs Intelligence
AI Cohort Risk Alerts

Detect learners falling behind

Identify disengagement patterns

Recommend early intervention

AI Training Needs Capture

Analyze performance data across cohorts

Identify common skill gaps

Feed insights into future course planning

6. AI Feedback & Sentiment Analysis

Analyzes qualitative feedback at scale.

Sources:

Course reviews

Ratings

Open-text feedback

Support tickets

Outputs:

Sentiment trends

Recurring complaints

Highlighted improvement opportunities

7. AI Discussion Moderation

Supports safe and productive learning environments.

Capabilities:

Detect inappropriate or off-topic content

Flag posts for moderator review

Suggest automated or drafted moderator responses

8. AI Support Triage

Optimizes support operations by:

Classifying incoming support requests

Suggesting replies

Routing issues to the correct team or faculty member

Prioritizing urgent learner problems

9. AI-Assisted Localization

Supports global delivery of DTMA programs.

Capabilities:

Translate course content

Suggest culturally appropriate phrasing

Assist with multilingual support responses

Maintain consistency across localized versions
